Disover the city in a different way

Roll up and step this way for the most alternative activities to do in Amsterdam.!

Verse

Prinsengracht 581, 1016 HT Amsterdam

For a well-curated edit of sustainable clothing, vegan leather goods and fine jewellery which champions local designers, visit the newly opened Verse. Housed on the ground floor of a restored canal house on the picturesque Prinsengracht, it also has a coffee and smoothie bar.

The Port Game

Moermanskkade 71, 1013 Amsterdam

If you want to work out your brain as well, be sure to check out The Port Game for a thrilling mix of creativity, puzzles, and adrenaline.

Skins Cosmetics

Runstraat 11, 1016 GJ Amsterdam

Around the corner is Skins, a beauty mecca housing cosmetics from RMS, Kjaer Weis and Ellis Faas, skin treats from Aesop and Susanne Kaufmann, as well as a lust-inducing selection of candles and fragrances by Diptyque, Byredo and Frederic Maille.

Telephone

Athenaeum Boekhandel

Spui 14-16, 1012 XA Amsterdam

. In a typically narrow Amsterdam house on the corner of the rambling Spuistraat is the homely Athenaeum Boekhandel. Stocking a fine collection of independent titles, the staff are happy to oblige even the most far-fetched reading requests.
