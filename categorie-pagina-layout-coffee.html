Coffee spots in Amsterdam

Amsterdam is packed with hot spots. We know how hard it can be to find the perfect place to have your perfect latte at. We're here to help!

Lot Sixty One

Kinkerstraat 112, 1053 ED Amsterdam

They serve what is widely considered to be the city’s best coffee from its tiny roastery-meets-café on Kinkerstraat. Its famed beans are sold by many Amsterdam coffee shops, but it’s also worth looking up.

Two for Joy

Haarlemmerdijk 182, 1013 JK Amsterdam

Two for Joy is located on Haarlemmerstraat, the vibrant stretch of road dotted with vintage boutiques, curiosity shops and the city’s oldest cinema.

Dignita

Koninginneweg 218, 1075 EL Amsterdam

The bright, friendly Dignita in the city’s south has a brunch which is definitely worth travelling for.

Walter White's Espresso

Czaar Peterstraat 21, Amsterdam

A roving espresso bar from enthusiastic and impassioned Amsterdammer, Nico. The convivial atmosphere that comes with any of Nico’s coffees are perhaps the finest example of gezellig.
